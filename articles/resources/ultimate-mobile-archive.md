# Ultimate Roblox Mobile Archive

*Description: Browse and download historical versions of Roblox for iOS and Android*
*Credits: Yakov5776 (site), SonicGamingTV2019 (IPA maintenance)*

Online archive with an interactive viewer for browsing and downloading historical Roblox mobile clients.

- [Ultimate Roblox Mobile Archive](https://yakov5776.github.io/ultimaterobloxmobilearchive/)
- Supports iOS and Android
- Download individual IPAs or split APK bundles
- Contribute decrypted IPAs via the site

IPAs on this site will always remain free.
